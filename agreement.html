Scope of Work

On Point Digital Studio ("Provider") agrees to deliver the following services for Fitzgerald's Irish Tavern ("Client"):

  • Custom website design and development (single-page home + full menu page)
  • Responsive design optimized for mobile, tablet, and desktop
  • Domain transfer from GoDaddy to Porkbun (at client's request)
  • DNS configuration and Cloudflare CDN setup
  • SSL certificate (included with Cloudflare, free of charge)
  • Google Analytics setup and integration
  • Schema markup (LocalBusiness + Restaurant structured data)
  • SEO-optimized meta tags, headings, and page structure

Pricing

One-Time Website Build $800.00
Monthly Hosting & Maintenance $25.00/mo

No upfront payment required. Build fee is due upon client satisfaction.
Monthly hosting begins after the site goes live. Payments processed via Stripe.

What's Included in Monthly Hosting

Included ($25/mo)

  • Cloudflare Pages hosting (global CDN, DDoS protection, 99.9% uptime)
  • SSL certificate maintenance
  • Design changes, text updates, and photo swaps — completed within 24 to 48 hours
  • Domain registration and renewal (covered by Provider at no cost to Client)
  • Monthly uptime monitoring

Not Included

  • Content creation or copywriting beyond initial build
  • Photography or video production

  • Client owns their domain name and all original content at all times.
  • Client may request and receive all website files at any time.
  • If Client cancels hosting, Provider will deliver a complete copy of all site files within 7 business days.
  • Provider retains the right to display the completed work in its portfolio and marketing materials.

Timeline

  • Estimated completion: 1 to 2 weeks from receipt of all required assets.
  • Timeline is dependent on Client providing: menu updates, photos, and design feedback in a timely manner.
  • Provider will communicate progress and request feedback at key milestones.

Payment Terms

  • The one-time build fee of $800.00 is due only after Client is satisfied with the completed website.
  • Monthly hosting of $25.00 begins on the date the site goes live and is billed on the same date each month.
  • All payments are processed securely through Stripe.
  • If a monthly payment fails, Provider will attempt to reach Client before taking any action on the site.

Termination

  • Either party may cancel this agreement with 30 days written notice (email is fine).
  • There are no long-term contracts. Hosting is month-to-month after the initial build.
  • Upon cancellation, Client receives all site files within 7 business days at no additional charge.
  • Any outstanding balance for work already completed remains due upon termination.

Provider is not liable for any loss of revenue, data, or business opportunities arising from website downtime caused by third-party services (Cloudflare, domain registrars, etc.).

Any changes to this agreement must be agreed upon in writing by both parties. An email exchange confirming the change is sufficient.
