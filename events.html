Finnegan's Wake 2027

Every year, Fitzgerald's hosts Finnegan's Wake — our legendary St. Patrick's Day tradition and the biggest celebration in New Port Richey and all of Pasco County. Hundreds of people flood downtown for a day of Irish pride, live music, green beer, and pure craic.

The countdown is on. Mark your calendar — March 17, 2027.
