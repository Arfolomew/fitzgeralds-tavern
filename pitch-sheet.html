Hosting: GoDaddy vs. On Point

Your domain is currently registered and hosted through GoDaddy. Here's what changes when we take over hosting.

GoDaddy

  • Builder limits what you can customize
  • Shared hosting, your site competes for resources
  • Older HTTP/1.1 protocol (slower)
  • Generic support, not specific to your site
  • Constant upsells for SSL, backups, SEO tools
  • Renewal prices jump after the first year

On Point

  • Cloudflare global edge network (300+ locations)
  • Free automatic SSL (always secure)
  • HTTP/2 and HTTP/3 for faster loads
  • Built-in DDoS protection and security
  • Unlimited bandwidth, no traffic caps
  • Direct line to the people who built your site
  • No hidden fees, one simple monthly price
