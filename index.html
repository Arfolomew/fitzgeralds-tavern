Our Story

A New Port Richey Tradition Since 2004

For over two decades, Fitzgerald's Irish Tavern has been the heart of downtown New Port Richey. From our signature fish and chips to craft cocktails on the patio, we're where the community comes together — for great food, cold pints, and unforgettable nights.

Whether you're catching the game on one of our 26 HDTVs or enjoying live music under the stars, there's always a seat at our table.
